# Product Guide

## Project Goal
A comprehensive Digital Menu system and configurable Admin Dashboard for restaurants. The platform allows restaurant owners to generate and customize their websites based on subscription plans, and seamlessly manage their dishes, categories, and online presence.

## Target Audience
- **Local Eateries:** Small to medium-sized restaurants needing an accessible, fully-featured digital menu and an easy-to-manage online storefront.

## Key Features
- **Admin Dashboard:** A centralized, user-friendly panel for restaurant owners to edit menus, categories, and customize their site template based on their plan.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Direct integration for orders, streamlining customer communication and simplifying the checkout flow.
- **Template Personalization:** Configurable templates customized to different restaurant needs (e.g., pizzeria, bakery) driven by a seamless static injection architecture.

## Value Proposition
- **All-in-One Management:** Providing restaurant owners with a unified platform that handles their online menu, order flow (via WhatsApp), and website presentation without requiring technical expertise.
